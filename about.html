ABOUT US

Our Restaurant

Centrally located on the main street in chennai on Main Tnagar offers a delightful mix of traditional Tamilian dishes infused with modern flavors. Chef Kandasamy embraces the “farm to table” approach with locally sourced farm fresh meats and vegetables as well as the finest imported traditional ingredients that define Tamil cuisine. Our restaurant is the perfect place for business lunches and dinners, intimate affairs, family dinners and a great place for special events and catering. We also take reservations for parties of up to 8 people.

Parking and Public Transportation

We offer three hour free parking at the newly built Pearl Street parking garage, and there is plenty of street parking after business hours. We are also one block away from the central Train Station.

Open for Lunch and Dinner

We serve lunch and dinner seven days a week. See our Menus page to view our full lunch and dinner menus. Our business hours are:

  • Monday to Friday: 11:30am to 10pm
  • Saturday: 12pm to 10pm
  • Sunday: 12pm to 9pm
